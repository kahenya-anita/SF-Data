Cost share by part tier – % of total monthly spend

1-part 2-part 3-part+
3-part+ drives 88–94% of cost. 2-part cost share grew from 5.4% to 11.2%.
3-part+ responses drive nearly all costs: Across all three months, the 3-part+ tier accounts for 88–94% of total spend. Even as volume more than doubled, the average cost per tracked part held stable at ~GHS 0.089–0.093 — indicating part composition is consistent despite scale.
Total costs grew 48.7% across the quarter: From GHS 480.57 in December to GHS 714.63 in February, with a growth in agent message volume of 109%. The 2-part cost tier grew fastest in share from GHS 26.16 (5.4% of spend) to GHS 80.04 (11.2%) as shorter responses scale with volume.
